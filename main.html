Inception (2010) is a sci-fi thriller directed by Christopher Nolan. It follows Dom Cobb, a skilled thief who enters people’s dreams to steal secrets. Cobb is offered a chance to clear his criminal record if he can accomplish the impossible task of planting an idea inside someone’s subconscious — a process known as “inception.”

Main Actors

  • Leonardo DiCaprio as Dom Cobb
  • Joseph Gordon-Levitt as Arthur
  • Elliot Page as Ariadne
  • Tom Hardy as Eames
  • Ken Watanabe as Saito
  • Cillian Murphy as Robert Fischer
